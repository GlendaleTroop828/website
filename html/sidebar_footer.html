Working with Young Men for More Than 25 Years!
Our Mission
We provide a quality boy run scouting program that enriches the lives of our boys and prepares them to be responsible citizens of the world. We do this by equipping our young men with the tools they need to make moral and ethical choices throughout their lifetimes based on the values of the scout law, oath, slogan, motto and outdoor code. Enrollment
We are a small close knit troop with roughly 20 scouts between the ages of 11 and 17.   We have dedicated adult leaders and parents that help our troop run smoothly and efficiently.  Over the years, our troop has advanced twenty five boys to the rank of Eagle.  Leadership
We strongly encourage our boys to take on leadership roles in the troop.  We believe one of the most important aspects of scouting is this opportunity and the training that goes along with it.
