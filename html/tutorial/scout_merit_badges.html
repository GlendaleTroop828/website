• Click the Advancement > Scouts BSA Advancement link in the Troopmaster menu bar.
  • Click the view button next to your scouts name.


  • You can click on any of the boxes to get more info on Rank/Merit Badges/Special Awards.
  • Start by clicking on Completed Merit Badges.


  • This will give you a list of all completed merit badges, as well as which ranks they can be applied to. The R/P check boxes list whether they have been Reported to Council and Presented to the Scout


  • Going back, now click on the Partial Merit Badges link
  • This will give you an overview of any merit badges that have been started, but not completed (as long as the scout has notified the advancement chair)
  • Clicking the name of a merit badge will give you details for unfinished requirements.
