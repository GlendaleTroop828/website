• Group emails are a benefit of the new troop website. You can send emails via troopmaster or with your favorite email app.
  • To use an external email app, just send your emails to the group email addresses that can be found on the Contact List page.
  • To use Troopmaster to send your emails, select Email > Email from the Troopmaster menu bar.
  • You will then be asked to verify your email address (one time).


  • You can choose individual recipients by type (adults, scouts, leaders), or if you click the drop-down menu, you can choose EmailGroups to send an email out to everyone in that group.
