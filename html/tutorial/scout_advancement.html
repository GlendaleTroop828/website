• Click the Advancement > Scouts BSA Advancement link in the Troopmaster menu bar.
  • Click the view button next to your scouts name.


  • You can click on any of the boxes to get more info on Rank/Merit Badges/Special Awards.
  • Start by clicking on your scout's current rank.


  • This will give you an overview of what your scout has already completed, as well as what they need to be working on to advance in rank. Some of the information auto-populates (service hours, nights-camping, etc...) based on activities that the scouts attend. The rest of the information will get entered upon request or after board of reviews.
